About Us

The Japanese Club is a community for students and enthusiasts passionate about Japanese language, culture, and traditions. We organize cultural events, language exchanges, workshops, and fun activities to share our love for Japan.

Practice Japanese conversation with fellow learners and native speakers.

Workshops
